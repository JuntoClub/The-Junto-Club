How It Works

Win Surprise Set Envelopes during live Junto Club shows on Whatnot. Every Surprise Set Envelope you win — Gold or Silver — counts as one toward your total. Win 12 Surprise Set Envelopes and you earn one Free Pack of The Chase — shipping on us.

Your Reward

  • Every 12 Surprise Set Envelopes won earns 1 Free Pack of The Chase, from thechase.info. We cover shipping.
  • The reward repeats: 24 won earns 2 free, 36 earns 3, and so on, as long as you keep winning within your active window.

The 30-Day Window

  • You have 30 days from your first Surprise Set Envelope won to reach 12.
  • When you reach 12 and your Free Pack is earned, your count resets to zero and a fresh 30-day window begins with your next Surprise Set Envelope won.
  • If you do not reach 12 within your 30-day window, your count resets to zero and those envelopes are forfeited.

Earning & the Leaderboard

  • Surprise Set Envelopes won are credited to your Whatnot username after each show.
  • The leaderboard is updated after shows, not in real time. The “Last updated” date at the top shows when it was last refreshed.

Accounts

  • Your total is tied to a single Whatnot account and has no cash value.
  • Surprise Set Envelopes won cannot be transferred, sold, gifted, or combined across accounts.

Redemption

  • When you reach 12, contact us at info@juntoclubco.com to claim your Free Pack of The Chase. We cover shipping.

The Record

  • The Junto Club’s internal record is the official and final count. If there is ever a discrepancy between the leaderboard, a screenshot, a physical card, and our records, our records govern.
  • We reserve the right to correct errors, and to adjust or void Surprise Set Envelopes credited through error, fraud, chargebacks, or returned or cancelled purchases.

Changes

  • The Junto Club may update these rules or end the program at any time. Material changes will be posted on this page.
